## Details
Workday's VIBE Index (Value, Inclusion, Belonging, Equity) is a data-driven framework that measures:
- **Inclusion**: Creating environments where everyone feels welcome
- **Belonging**: Fostering connections and community
- **Fair access to opportunities**: Ensuring equitable access across the employee lifecycle

Janet's award recognized her exceptional contributions to:
- **Community Building**: Founded and scaled Employee Belonging Council globally
- **Cultural Impact**: Created inclusive spaces and programs
- **Leadership**: Demonstrated leadership in diversity, equity, and inclusion initiatives

The award is Workday's internal global recognition for Product & Technology organization, highlighting the importance of creating inclusive workplace cultures.
