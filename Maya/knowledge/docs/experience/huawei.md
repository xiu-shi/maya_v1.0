## About Huawei
Huawei is a global Chinese company that provides information and communications technology (ICT) infrastructure and smart devices, including smartphones, laptops, and wearables. Founded in 1987, the company offers a wide range of products and solutions across its consumer, enterprise, and carrier business lines, and is particularly known for its telecommunications equipment and contributions to 5G technology.

**Business Areas:**
- Consumer Devices: Smartphones, PCs, tablets, and wearable devices
- Enterprise Business: ICT infrastructure and solutions for various industries (finance, manufacturing, transportation)
- Carrier Business: Major supplier of telecommunications equipment and services for mobile and broadband networks
- Cloud Services: Cloud computing products and solutions

**Global Presence:**
- Founded in 1987 by Ren Zhengfei in Shenzhen, China
- Technologies connect a large portion of the world's population
- Used in over 170 countries
- Significant R&D investment, holding large number of patents in 5G and ICT
- World leader in telecommunications equipment and prominent position in 5G technology
