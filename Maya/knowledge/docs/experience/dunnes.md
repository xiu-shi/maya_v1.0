## About Dunnes Stores
Dunnes Stores launched in Ireland 80 years ago and revolutionised the concept of retail in Ireland by promising "Better Value" to its customers. A family business, then and now, they take pride in continuing to re-imagine, challenge and evolve their "Better Value" promise in response to customers' changing needs.

**Better choice and better value in food, fashion & homewares:**
- Food: Unique range combining Dunnes Stores' own brand of fresh, local produce along with specialist exclusive lines such as award-winning "Simply Better" range
- Clothing and homeware: Choices to suit different lifestyles and budgets, guaranteed affordable quality
- Big supporter of Irish talent, creating collections with leading Irish designers in fashion and homeware
- Collections include exclusive brands like Savida and Gallery
- Stores: Continues to revolutionise the role retail plays in customers' lives, reimagining shopping experience to inspire and enrich customers' lives
