## About Woodie's
Woodie's is Ireland's market-leading, 100% Irish-owned DIY, home, and garden retailer, with 35 branches nationwide and an online store. They offer a wide range of products, including DIY, paints, lighting, homestyle, housewares, and gardening supplies, aiming to help customers improve their homes and gardens.

**Key Information:**
- Ownership: 100% Irish-owned and a subsidiary of Grafton Group plc
- Services: Comprehensive range of DIY, home improvement, and gardening products
- Product Range: Paints, lighting, homestyle items, housewares, bathroom accessories, building products, gardening supplies, and fitted kitchens
- Market Position: Ireland's market leader in the DIY, home, and garden sector
- Presence: 35 physical stores across Ireland and online store at Woodies.ie
- History: First retail operation opened in 1987 in Walkinstown, Dublin

**Services and Values:**
- Customer-focused mission to help people make their homes and gardens look and feel special
- Staff development through Skillnet Ireland collaboration
- Online & in-store convenience with 4-hour click and collect and standard delivery
- Free and easy returns within 30 days
- Sustainability: Old WEEE electrical items can be returned for recycling
